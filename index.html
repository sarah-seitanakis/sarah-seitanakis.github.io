ABOUT

Getting acquainted

I have just finished earning my Bachelor's degree from Allegheny College with a major in Physics and a double minor in Studio Art and Mathematics. My undergraduate research focused on Computational Astrophysics. In fact, the goal of my senior thesis was to model collisions between black holes and stars using a smoothed partical hydrodynamics code. I am also passionate about art. I have spent many hours in Allegheny's Art building, and I was thrilled to hold membership in the community of artists who walk those halls. I loved my time as an undergraduate, and I am looking forward to turning the page to the next chapter.

Since I am newly graduated, I am currently looking for a job— hopefully, one where I can utilize the creative and computational skillsets that I have built throughout my education. I hope to find a career path that allows me to continue to solve complex problems and work with a team.
